Higher or Lower

Welcome to the "Higher or Lower" game! The objective is simple: you need to predict whether the next card will be of a higher or lower value than the current card.

- If you believe the next card is higher, select "Higher" (input 1).

- If you believe the next card is lower, select "Lower" (input 0).

Each round, you'll receive points for correct guesses, and the game continues until you decide to stop!
