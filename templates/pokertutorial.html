How to Play Poker

Poker is a popular card game that combines skill, strategy, and luck. The objective is to win chips by either having the best hand or convincing other players to fold.

Basic Rules

  • Each player is dealt a hand of cards.
  • Players can bet, call, raise, or fold based on their cards.
  • The best five-card hand wins the pot.

Hands Ranking

  1. Royal Flush (T, K, Q, J, A all same suit)
  2. Straight Flush (5 cards in a row all same suit)
  3. Four of a Kind
  4. Full House (2 of the same type, 3 of the same type)
  5. Flush (5 cards same suit)
  6. Straight (5 Cards in a row)
  7. Three of a Kind
  8. Two Pair (Two pairs of cards that are the same type)
  9. One Pair (A pair of two cards)
  10. High Card
